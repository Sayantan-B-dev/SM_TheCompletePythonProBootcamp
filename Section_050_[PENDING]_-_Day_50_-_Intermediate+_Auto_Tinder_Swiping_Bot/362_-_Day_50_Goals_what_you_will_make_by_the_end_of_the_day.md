### Why This Stands Out

Government press releases provide structured, authoritative, timestamped data that is extremely valuable for analytics, trend monitoring, and NLP projects. The content is public-domain in most cases and explicitly intended for redistribution. Legal risk is minimal when scraping responsibly and respecting `robots.txt`.

### Data Quality Characteristics

| Attribute               | Value    |
| ----------------------- | -------- |
| Structure Consistency   | High     |
| Update Frequency        | Daily    |
| Legal Risk              | Very Low |
| Analytical Value        | High     |
| Authentication Required | No       |

### What You Can Build

* Real-time policy trend tracker.
* NLP-based sentiment or sector classification model.
* Automated summary generator for economic updates.
* Time-series database of ministry announcements.

### Technical Depth

* Pagination crawling.
* Structured metadata extraction.
* Timestamp normalization.
* Incremental scraping logic.
